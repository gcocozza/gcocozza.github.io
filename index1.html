Localization and translation services

Globalize your business. Localize your app, software or website.



The concepts of localization and translation are difficult to distinguish from one another, even professionals in the industry have a hard time at differentiating them. It is important to acknowlegde, though, that both go together. On one hand, localization refers to the process of adapting a product to a particular locale, and that process includes the actual task of translating. On the other hand, the translation of a website, for example, refers only to the text that a website stores (excluding media content).

If your business plan is to pursue a global market, then you might be very interested in the localization of your end-user content. It is also very likely that you might be interested in localizing not only to one language, but in several pairs depending on your target audience. Marketing studies have already proven that customers who have access to content in their native language have a higher likelihood to buy. The localization of a website might include, descriptions of products, manuals, delivery methods, etc.

Set below is a list the most common tasks that a localization of a product involves:

  • TRANSLATION OF TEXTS
  • LOCALIZATION OF IMAGES AND VIDEOS
  • DESIGN OF ACCESSIBLE CONTENT
  • IMPLEMENTATION OF SEO STRATEGIES
  • DESKTOP PUBLISHING
  • EDITING OF LOCALIZATION FORMAT STANDARDS: .xml, .html, .html5, .ini, .exe, .po, .json, .xliff, javascript, etc.
