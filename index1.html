About me

Shortly after graduating from the University of Costa Rica with a BA in English, I decided to set off for Europe. It is a continent that first appealed me for its historical richness and its railway systems. It has been a fascinating year, in which I have learned basic German at the University of Bamberg and improved my Italian to an intermediate level in Calabria, Italy. I strongly support the idea that these traveling experiences add as much significance to the preparation of a successful translator as those academic ones. For this, as a recently enrolled student in the Master's Degree in Tradumatics, at the University of Barcelona, I continue feeding my interest in languages and cultures by learning Catalan, while acquiring new skills and knowledge that will allow me to face with confidence the growing challenges in translation technologies. 

"I care about my clients. I like to offer a service that suits the clients' needs, a service that is confidential, at a fair rate and on time."
